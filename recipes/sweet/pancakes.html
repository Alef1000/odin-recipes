Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of flower
  • Oil/butter (for pan)

Fire Up The Stoves!

  1. Mix the wet and dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Put a pan on the stove at medium heat and cover in oil or butter (based on preference)
  3. Pour the batter over the pan as thins as possible. This is what will give it it's distinctive crêpe-like texture and taste!
  4. Take the pancakes out of the pan when cooked through and cover with whatever toppings you'd like!
  5. Now roll the crêp...I mean pancakes and enjoy!
